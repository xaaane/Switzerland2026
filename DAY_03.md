## DAY 3: Journey to Wengen & Exploring Mürren

### September 10th, 2026

**Trip Overview**

Today you will journey from the Lake Lucerne region deep into the heart of the Bernese Oberland. Your destination is the serene, car-free alpine village of Wengen. After checking in, you'll spend the afternoon exploring the stunning cliff-side villages of Mürren and Gimmelwald, which offer dramatic views of the surrounding peaks.

---

### **Schedule**

**Morning (Flexible): Breakfast & Check-out in Lucerne**

*   **Action:** Enjoy a final breakfast in Lucerne.
*   **Action:** Check out of your hotel.

**10:05 AM - 11:55 AM: The Luzern-Interlaken Express**

*   **The Journey:** Board the Luzern-Interlaken Express train to Interlaken Ost.
*   **Tip:** For the best scenery, find a seat on the **right side** of the train in the direction of travel.

**11:55 AM - 12:21 PM: Transfer to Lauterbrunnen**

*   **Action:** At Interlaken Ost station, change to the Berner Oberland-Bahn (BOB) train heading towards **Lauterbrunnen**.

**12:21 PM - 1:00 PM: Ascend to Wengen & Check-in**

*   **Cogwheel Train:** At Lauterbrunnen station, board the green and yellow Wengernalpbahn cogwheel train for the steep 15-minute ascent to Wengen.
*   **Action:** Arrive in Wengen, find your hotel, check in, and drop off your luggage.

**1:00 PM - 2:00 PM: Lunch in Wengen**

*   **Dining:** Enjoy a relaxing lunch at a restaurant in Wengen before your afternoon excursion.

**2:00 PM - 6:30 PM: Exploring Mürren & Gimmelwald**

*   **Descend & Ascend:** Take the train from Wengen down to Lauterbrunnen. Cross the street to the Grütschalp cable car (Lauterbrunnen BLM), ride it up to Grütschalp, and take the scenic train to Mürren.
*   **Explore Mürren:** Walk through the beautiful village, taking in the dramatic mountain views.
*   **Hike to Gimmelwald:** Take the highly recommended, easy 30-minute downhill walk from Mürren to the rustic hamlet of Gimmelwald.
*   **Return Journey:** From Gimmelwald, take the Schilthornbahn cable car down to Stechelberg, then the PostBus along the valley floor back to Lauterbrunnen, and finally, the cogwheel train back up to Wengen.

**7:30 PM Onwards: Dinner in Wengen**

*   **Dining:** Enjoy dinner in the peaceful atmosphere of Wengen.

---

### **Practical Information & Tips**

*   **Swiss Travel Pass:** Your pass covers this entire day of travel for free, from Lucerne all the way through the Mürren-Gimmelwald loop and back to Wengen.
*   **Luggage Transfer:** Consider using the SBB luggage service to send your main bags from Lucerne to Wengen to make your travel day easier.
*   **The Mürren-Gimmelwald Walk:** This short walk is highly recommended over taking the cable car between the two villages.
*   **Check Timetables:** Be aware of the time for the last train back up to Wengen from Lauterbrunnen to ensure you don't miss it.
