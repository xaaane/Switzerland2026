## DAY 4: Rothorn, Brienz & Giessbach Falls (Timed Itinerary)

**Trip Overview**

This is an ambitious and action-packed travel day that combines the best of the Lake Brienz region, built around a precise schedule of crucial departures. Starting with a dawn departure from Wengen, you will store your luggage in Interlaken before ascending the Brienzer Rothorn on a historic steam train. You'll then have time to explore Brienz village before taking a boat and funicular to the magnificent Giessbach Falls. Finally, you will retrieve your luggage and complete the journey to your new alpine base in Grindelwald for a late dinner.

---

### **Schedule**

**7:00 AM - 8:00 AM: Dawn Departure from Wengen**

*   **Action:** A very early start is essential. Check out of your hotel in Wengen and take the necessary train connections (Wengen -> Lauterbrunnen -> Interlaken Ost) to arrive at **Interlaken Ost by 8:00 AM**.

**8:00 AM - 8:15 AM: Luggage Storage at Interlaken Ost**

*   **Action:** Go directly to the luggage lockers or the staffed luggage counter to quickly deposit your main bags for the day.

**8:17 AM - 8:34 AM: Train to Brienz**

*   **Action:** Catch a train from Interlaken Ost that arrives in Brienz before 8:36 AM. This is critical for the day's timing. The Brienzer Rothorn station is directly across from the main train station.

**8:36 AM - 12:15 PM: Brienzer Rothorn Adventure**

*   **8:36 AM (DEPARTURE):** Depart from Brienz on the **first Brienzer Rothorn Bahn steam train**. The scenic journey up the mountain takes approximately one hour.
*   **~9:36 AM - 11:15 AM (SUMMIT TIME):** You have roughly **1 hour and 40 minutes** at the Rothorn Kulm summit. This is an excellent amount of time to walk the ridge paths, take in the phenomenal 360-degree views, and have a coffee or early lunch at the summit restaurant.
*   **11:15 AM (DEPARTURE):** Depart from Rothorn Kulm on the **return train**, arriving back in Brienz around 12:15 PM.

**12:15 PM - 1:40 PM: Lunch and Exploration in Brienz**

*   **Action:** You have a solid **1 hour and 25 minutes** to spend in Brienz.
*   **Dining:** Head to **Bino's Pizzeria** for lunch.
*   **Explore:** After lunch, you will have time for a focused walk along the lakeside promenade and up a portion of the famous **Brunngasse**.

**1:40 PM - 5:09 PM: Giessbach Falls Excursion**

*   **1:40 PM (DEPARTURE):** Depart from the Brienz ferry terminal on the boat to the **"Giessbach See"** landing.
*   **Explore:** Upon arrival, take the historic funicular up to the Grandhotel. You have a generous **~2.5 hours** to explore the Giessbach Falls, walk the path behind the waterfall, and relax at the historic hotel.
*   **5:09 PM (DEPARTURE):** Take the **return ferry** from Giessbach See, which will get you back to Brienz around 5:20 PM.

**~5:30 PM - 6:00 PM: Train to Interlaken Ost**

*   **Action:** Walk to the Brienz train station and take the next available train to Interlaken Ost.

**6:00 PM - 7:00 PM: Final Journey to Grindelwald**

*   **Action:** At Interlaken Ost, retrieve your luggage from storage.
*   **Action:** Board the Berner Oberland-Bahn (BOB) train for the final 30-minute scenic ride up to your new base, **Grindelwald**. Upon arrival, find your hotel and check in.

**8:00 PM Onwards: Late Dinner in Grindelwald**

*   **Dining:** After an incredible day, settle in and head out for a well-deserved late dinner.

---

### **Practical Information & Tips**

*   **Book in Advance:** It is highly recommended to book your **Brienzer Rothorn Bahn tickets online in advance** to secure your spot on the 8:36 AM train.
*   **Swiss Travel Pass:** Your pass covers the trains (Wengen-Interlaken-Brienz, Interlaken-Grindelwald) and the boat to Giessbach. It provides a significant discount on the Brienzer Rothorn Bahn and the Giessbach Funicular.
*   **Be Punctual:** This itinerary depends on making the specified departures for the Rothorn train and the ferry. Arrive at the stations/docks a few minutes early.
*   **Flexibility:** The time in Brienz (12:15 PM - 1:40 PM) is your main flexible window. If you'd rather spend more time on the promenade than on a long lunch, you can adjust accordingly.
