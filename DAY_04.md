## DAY 4: Exploring Wengen & Lauterbrunnen

### September 11th, 2026

**Trip Overview**

This day is dedicated to exploring the stunning Lauterbrunnen Valley. After a final morning in Wengen, you'll descend into the valley to witness its iconic waterfalls. At the end of the day, you will travel to your new alpine base in Grindelwald, checking in for the next two nights.

---

### **Schedule**

**9:00 AM - 11:00 AM: Exploring Wengen Village**

*   **Action:** Enjoy a leisurely breakfast at your hotel and check out. Leave your luggage with the hotel for the day.
*   **Action:** Spend the morning exploring car-free Wengen. Walk along the main street, browse the shops, and be sure to find the scenic viewpoint by the protestant church for a classic photo over the Lauterbrunnen Valley.

**11:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Descent to Lauterbrunnen**

*   **Action:** Before heading down, return to your hotel to collect your luggage.
*   **Action:** Take the cogwheel train from Wengen down into the valley, arriving at Lauterbrunnen. Store your luggage in the lockers at Lauterbrunnen station.

**12:00 PM - 1:30 PM: Lunch in Lauterbrunnen**

*   **Dining:** Enjoy lunch at a restaurant in Lauterbrunnen village.

**1:30 PM - 4:30 PM: Valley & Waterfall Exploration**

*   **Staubbach Falls:** From the village, walk to the iconic **Staubbach Falls**, which plunges nearly 300 meters from the cliff face. You can even walk up a short path to go behind the waterfall.
*   **Trümmelbach Falls:** From Lauterbrunnen, either take a scenic 30-minute walk or a short bus ride further down the valley to the **Trümmelbach Falls**. This is a series of ten glacial waterfalls hidden inside a mountain, made accessible by a tunnel lift and a network of platforms. It's a powerful and unique experience.

**4:30 PM - 5:30 PM: Travel to Grindelwald**

*   **Action:** Take the bus from Trümmelbach Falls back to Lauterbrunnen station.
*   **Action:** Collect your luggage from the lockers.
*   **Action:** Board the train to Grindelwald. This involves a short ride to Zweilütschinen, where you will change trains for the scenic journey up to Grindelwald.

**5:30 PM Onwards: Check-in and Relax**

*   **Action:** Arrive in Grindelwald, walk to your hotel, and check in.

**7:00 PM Onwards: Dinner in Grindelwald**

*   **Dining:** Enjoy dinner at a restaurant of your choice in Grindelwald.

---

### **Practical Information & Tips**

*   **Luggage Transfer:** The key to this day is managing your luggage. Using the lockers at Lauterbrunnen station is the most efficient way to enjoy the valley unencumbered. Make sure you have coins for the lockers.
*   **Swiss Travel Pass:** Your pass covers all train and bus travel for this day: Wengen to Lauterbrunnen, the bus to Trümmelbach Falls, and the train from Lauterbrunnen to Grindelwald. It also provides a discount on the Trümmelbach Falls entrance fee.
*   **Trümmelbach Falls:** It can be cool and misty inside the falls, so a light rain jacket is recommended. The stone paths can also be slippery, so wear shoes with good grip.
*   **Walking:** The walk from Lauterbrunnen to Trümmelbach is flat, easy, and scenic if you prefer it over the bus.
